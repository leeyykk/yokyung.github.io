Profound Poet

Reference Poem for Novice Poets

A Python NLP project that focuses on traditional NLP approaches to generate reference poems for novice poets. Poems are generated with the aggregation of various corpora, literary techinques, and human-optimized weights. Although the resultant poems are not well-furnished, it is a diligent approach to inspect and examine natural language in its most rudimentary yet meaningful sense.
