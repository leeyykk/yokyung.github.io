Talk Light

Make your dates, meetings, and conversations more lively, memorable and beautiful

Let us suppose that you are in a meeting with a group of people and you would like to visualize up to what proportion each side is participating in the conversation and encourage those who are not participating at all. This is where talklight comes in. All you need to do is to put this beautifully designed device in the middle of the table and watch it do its magic in a visually pleasing manner. The device features functionalities in which if it sees only one side speaking, it will encourage the silent side to speak more. If it sees everyone quiet then it will encourage everyone to speak up and break the ice. You can also save your special conversation as a unique aesthetic display.
