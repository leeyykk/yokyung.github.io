Early Bird

Wake Up! Run! Dominate the Sticker Board! Win the Bet!

Many feel discouraged from continuous failure of establishing early wake-up routines. Utilizing the synergy between gamification and aesthetics, Earlybird aids users to pursue and sustain an early wake-up routine. Gamification fosters motivation, and aesthetics can represent achievement and triumph in a friendly and personalized manner (unlike other applications that use number ranks)
